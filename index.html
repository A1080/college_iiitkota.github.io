ABOUT INSTITUTION

Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kota (IIIT, Kota) is an institute of national importance. IIIT Kota is a joint venture of the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) Govt. of India and Govt. of Rajasthan with Industry Partners in Public-Private-Partnership (PPP) mode. IIIT, Kota was established in 2013. Currently the institute is mentored by Malaviya National Institute of Technology Jaipur (MNIT Jaipur) and situated at MNIT Jaipur. The main site of IIIT, Kota shall be constructed at Ranpur near Kota on 100.37 acres of land made available by the Govt. of Rajasthan.
The institute is governed by the eminent personalities of the Government, Industry and Academia. Currently we offer B.Tech in the discipline of Computer Science & Engineering and Electronics & Communication Engineering only.

ADMISSION PROCEDURE

    INSTRUCTIONS TO CANDIDATES WHO ARE TAKING NEW ADMISSION IN B.TECH. PROGRAMME IN IIIT KOTA

  1. All the candidates who have accepted a seat in IIIT Kota during JoSAA rounds 1 to 6 / CSAB Special Rounds are required to Login to their account on JoSAA/CSAB portal to SECURE the provisional admission in IIIT Kota.
  2. It is mandatory for every candidate to pay JoSAA partial admission fee / CSAB seat acceptance fee.
  3. It is mandatory for every candidate, who wish to take admission in IIIT Kota, to pay the remaining Institute Fee. The remaining Institute Fee = Institute Fee – JoSAA Participation Fee – (JoSAA partial admission fee / CSAB seat acceptance fee) + JoSAA Processing Fee
  4. Take the receipt of JoSAA participation fee (35000/- or 15000/- as applicable) which you have already paid at the beginning of the JoSAA rounds. Also take the receipt of partial admission fee mentioned above in Step 2. Also take the receipt of remaining institute fee mentioned above in Step 3.
  5. Get ready with the self-attested scan copy of these three fee receipts. No hostel fee is required to pay.
  6. Get ready with the photocopies of all your of documents
  7. Make a single pdf of all the documents in the given order and send it to email ID: admissions@iiitkota.ac.in
  8. For any help, you can contact   1. Dr. Ankush Vashistha – 9529971483   2. Mr. Parag Somani – 9549650547

DEPARTMENTS

1-Computer Science and Engineering

Department of Computer Science and Engineering started functioning since July 2013.
Currently, the department offers a four-year B.Tech programme. Apart from the faculty members of the institute, the department is benefited from distinguished faculty of CSE department at MNIT Jaipur, our mentor institute. The department is in the process to get state-of-the-art labs to support the undergraduate program better.

2-Electronics and Communication Engineering

Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering started functioning since July 2015.
Currently, the department offers a four-year B.Tech programme. Apart from the faculty members of the institute, the department is benefited from distinguished faculty of ECE department at MNIT Jaipur. The department is in the process to get state-of-the-art labs to support the undergraduate program better.
